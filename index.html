About Messi

Lionel Messi, in full Lionel Andrés Messi, also called Leo Messi, born June 24, 1987, Rosario, Argentina, Argentine-born football player who received a record-setting seven Ballon d’Or awards as the world’s top male player (2009–12, 2015, 2019, and 2021). In 2022 he helped Argentina win the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA)’s World Cup.
